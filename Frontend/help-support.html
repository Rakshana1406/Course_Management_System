Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

Go to Browse Courses, choose your preferred course and click the Enroll button.

How can I track my learning progress?

Visit the Progress page to see completed modules, current progress, and certificates.

Where can I download study materials?

Open your enrolled course, then click Study Materials to access PDFs and notes.

How do I receive a certificate?

Complete all course modules and assessments successfully to earn your certificate.
